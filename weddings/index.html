Queenstown Wedding Entertainment

Make your special day unforgettable with Bourbon Sour, Queenstown's premier wedding band. Our versatile performances, ranging from relaxed acoustic sets to high-energy dance numbers, are perfect for every moment of your wedding.

Why Choose Us?

  • Seasoned musicians with many years of experience.
  • Customisable setlists to match your preferences.
  • High-quality sound and lighting equipment provided.
  • Unparalleled energy and engagement to get your guests on the dance floor.

Wedding Packages

  • Acoustic Ceremony Package

    Includes live acoustic music for the ceremony and a smooth transition to your cocktail hour. Perfect for creating a magical ambiance.

  • Reception Party Package

    High-energy live band for the reception, complete with professional sound and lighting. We'll have your guests dancing all night long.

  • Full-Day Entertainment Package
